Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2024

Cancellation

If you wish to cancel your project after paying the initial ₹999 booking amount, you must notify us before the actual development work begins (usually within 24-48 hours).

Refunds

If the project is cancelled before work commences, the ₹999 booking amount is 100% refundable. The refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 working days.

No refunds will be issued once the project development has fully started and resources have been allocated.
